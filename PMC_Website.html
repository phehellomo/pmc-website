Two Ways to Partner

Fund us directly, or fund the work directly — your choice.

The company invites institutions to provide funds or grants for the development, validation, testing, prototyping, and commercialisation of its innovations. This can happen in whichever manner the funding institution prefers — either as a direct disbursement, or through a partnership model in which the funder takes responsibility for paying engineers, specialists, IP attorneys, certification bodies, and all other service providers directly.

  • Where preferred, the funder pays directly as bridging finance, a loan, or an inventor's fee to the company.
  • In the partnership model, the funder covers the Director's salary, car allowance, and fuel allowance.
  • The funder also covers the accountant/admin salary, one project manager per project, and two general workers per project.
  • All engineers, specialists, IP attorneys, and certification costs are paid directly to the service providers — never through the company.

Our Objectives

Engineering solutions for South Africa's hardest infrastructure gaps.

Phehello Molise Consultants exists to research, patent, and commercialise practical technologies that close the gap between South Africa's infrastructure needs and what is currently available. Every innovation in the portfolio began as a real, lived problem — not a theoretical brief — which is why each one is built to be reliable, affordable, and immediately useful to the households and communities who need it most. The company's focus spans five priority areas:

Security

Developing intelligent, retrofit-friendly security and protective technologies that respond to real risks facing households, public spaces, farms, and critical assets.

Energy

Designing next-generation, AI-enhanced clean energy systems that reduce dependence on an unreliable grid and put resilient power within reach of homes, farms, and industry.

Digital Technology

Embedding AI, sensing, and digital intelligence into physical infrastructure so that systems monitor, adapt, and improve themselves over time — and resist the piracy and hacking risks facing ordinary households.

Water & Sanitation

Building low-cost, low-maintenance systems that recycle, generate, and purify water for households and communities where conventional infrastructure falls short.

A further, cross-cutting objective is the valorisation of waste — turning wastewater, organic, and other waste streams into reusable water, energy, or material value — together with continued research into other deep-technology solutions that serve South Africa's developmental priorities.

These are not abstract goals. They are the company's response to problems its own Director has lived through directly — and that is the motivation behind every patent in the portfolio: to do good, reliable work that solves practical, daily problems for South Africans.
